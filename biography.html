About us

Both Louise and Amy have undergone classical instrumental training under the guidance of the department at Maynooth University, and have recently achieved an honours degree in performance on piano and violin.


Louise has been studying in the Royal Irish Academy of Music for the past 9 years and is currently studying under Veronica Mc Sweeney, while Amy is studying violin under Sebastien Petiet. Both are experienced singers and musicians, having been part of both the Loreto and Maynooth University Choir.


Amy has toured around the world with the Cross Border Orchestra of Ireland and is currently a member of the Maynooth University Chamber Orchestra.
